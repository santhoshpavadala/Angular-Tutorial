Directives in Angular

What are Directives in Angular?

In Angular, directives are instructions in the DOM.
They tell Angular how to change the appearance or behavior of an element.

In short:
Directives = DOM manipulation + behavior control

  • Structural Directives
  • Attribute Directives
  • Custom Directives
